Hubbble

Introduction

Hubbble was designed during an 8 hour designathon held by Design Interactive at UC Davis. Serving as a platform for networking, community-building, and personal growth, Hubbble caters not only to designers but also to individuals looking to enhance their skills across various design-related domains. Key features of Hubbble include specialized Hubbble communities tailored to specific interests, a creative networking swipe feature, weekly challenges, and a mini-profile portfolio. Our aim with Hubbble was to foster a welcoming environment where users feel empowered to initiate conversations, sustain their networking connections, further their career skills, and contribute their expertise to support others.

My Role

In this group designathon, I served as one of the main designers and involved in a majority of the design process. We conducted interviews, wrote competitive audits, developed our design, and crafted an elevator pitch.

Specifically, I worked on conducting user interviews, brainstorming, initial design, final design, and prototyping.
